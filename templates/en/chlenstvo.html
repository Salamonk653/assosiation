Why it is profitable and important to adhere to the principles and values of
IAPEF and join membership?

Joining the community of progressive entrepreneurs of Kyrgyzstan through participation in the activities of the association IAPEF, businessmen get the opportunity to protect and promote their interests at the state and local levels, access to extensive contacts and connections in a business environment. Flexible policy in the activities of the association It is built taking into account gender aspects and is aimed at perceiving the most advanced world processes of conducting business. Membership in IAPEF helps to promote the successful development of the business environment in Kyrgyzstan to develop own national business standards.

To join the association you need to take 2 simple steps:


1) fill out the form, indicating: name, phone number, email address;


2) send the completed form. Our staff will contact you at within one hour
